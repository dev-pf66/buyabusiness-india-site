# About Buy a Business India

**Buy a Business India is a free educational resource helping aspiring business owners navigate the process of acquiring small and medium businesses in India.** We provide comprehensive guides, tools, and checklists to help you find, evaluate, and successfully purchase an existing business.

---

## What Is Our Mission?

**Our mission is to make business ownership accessible by providing clear, actionable guidance on buying existing businesses in India.** We believe that acquiring an established business offers a faster, lower-risk path to entrepreneurship than starting from scratch, and we want to help more people pursue this opportunity.

Starting a business from zero requires years of unprofitable operation while you develop products, build a customer base, and figure out what works. Most startups fail. Buying an existing business lets you skip this risky phase entirely—you acquire proven revenue, trained employees, and operational systems from day one. Yet despite these advantages, business acquisition remains poorly understood in India. Information is scattered, much of it is inaccurate, and professional advice is expensive. Buy a Business India exists to fill this gap with free, comprehensive, India-specific guidance.

| What We Believe | Why It Matters |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Acquisition > Starting for most people | Lower risk, immediate cash flow, proven model |
| Education should be free | Access shouldn't depend on ability to pay advisors |
| India-specific guidance matters | Global content misses local context |
| Practical beats theoretical | Checklists and tools, not just concepts |

## Who Creates This Content?

**Our content is created by people with direct experience in small business acquisitions, investment banking, and entrepreneurship in India.** We combine practical deal experience with research into best practices from global acquisition markets.

We're practitioners, not just writers. Our team has evaluated hundreds of small business opportunities, completed acquisitions, and advised business owners on exits. We know what works and what doesn't because we've done it. We also research extensively—studying how business acquisitions work in developed markets like the US, analyzing what separates successful acquisitions from failures, and adapting those lessons to Indian conditions. Every guide we publish reflects both real experience and ongoing learning.

**Our Content Principles:**

1. **Accuracy over speed** — We verify information and update content as laws and practices change
2. **Practical over theoretical** — Checklists, templates, and specific steps you can follow
3. **India-specific** — INR figures, Indian legal requirements, local marketplace names
4. **Conservative advice** — We'd rather you walk away from a marginal deal than overpay
5. **Transparent limitations** — We tell you when to hire professionals

## What We Don't Do

**We don't broker deals, provide legal or tax advice, or guarantee any outcomes.** Our content is educational. For professional advice specific to your situation, consult qualified lawyers, chartered accountants, and financial advisors.

This distinction matters. We can explain how business valuations work, but we can't tell you what a specific business is worth—that requires professional analysis of actual financials. We can outline due diligence best practices, but we can't conduct due diligence for you—that requires professionals examining real documents. We can describe typical deal structures, but we can't draft your purchase agreement—that requires a lawyer who understands your specific transaction. Use our content to become an informed buyer, then work with professionals to execute your deal.

| We Do | We Don't Do |
|-------|-------------|
| Explain valuation methods | Value specific businesses |
| Describe due diligence process | Conduct due diligence |
| Outline legal requirements | Provide legal advice |
| Share financing options | Arrange financing |
| Educate on best practices | Guarantee outcomes |

## How Is This Site Funded?

**Buy a Business India is independently operated without advertising, affiliate commissions, or sponsored content.** We may introduce premium resources or tools in the future, but all educational content remains free.

Independence matters because bias corrupts advice. Sites funded by brokers have incentive to encourage deals (more deals = more fees). Sites funded by advertisers have incentive for page views over accuracy. We have no such conflicts. Our only interest is providing accurate, helpful information. If we recommend considering seller financing, it's because seller financing often benefits buyers—not because someone pays us to say so. If we advise walking away from marginal deals, it's because that's genuinely good advice—not because we're selling an alternative.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is this site affiliated with any business brokers?

**No, Buy a Business India operates independently and has no affiliation with brokers, marketplaces, or advisory firms.** We mention platforms like SMERGERS, IndiaMart, and others because they're relevant resources, not because of any commercial relationship. We don't receive referral fees, advertising revenue, or any consideration for recommendations. This independence allows us to provide unbiased guidance. When we say certain marketplaces are better than others or recommend walking away from overpriced deals, you can trust that advice isn't influenced by commercial interests.

### Can you help me find a business to buy?

**We don't broker transactions or match buyers with sellers—our role is educational.** For finding businesses, we recommend using the platforms and strategies outlined in our [guide to finding businesses for sale](/guide/find-businesses/). Online marketplaces like SMERGERS and IndiaMart are good starting points. For more personalized assistance, consider engaging a business broker or M&A advisor in your target industry or geography. Our guides can help you work effectively with these professionals by ensuring you ask the right questions and evaluate opportunities properly.

### Are your guides legally accurate for India?

**Our guides reflect general practices and requirements in India as of the publication date, but laws and regulations change—always verify current requirements and consult qualified professionals for your specific situation.** We update content regularly and note last-updated dates on each page, but compliance requirements vary by state, industry, and transaction type. Tax rules change frequently. What was compliant last year may not be compliant today. Use our content as educational foundation, not legal advice. For any specific transaction, engage a lawyer and CA who can advise on current requirements.

### Do you offer consulting or advisory services?

**Currently, we focus on free educational content and don't offer paid consulting or advisory services.** The skills needed to write helpful guides differ from the skills needed to advise on specific transactions. For professional guidance on your acquisition, we recommend engaging specialists: CAs with M&A experience for financial due diligence and valuation, lawyers for legal review and documentation, and business brokers or advisors for deal sourcing and negotiation support. Our guides can help you find and work with these professionals effectively.
